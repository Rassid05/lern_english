Other English Verbs

Berikut adalah kumpulan kata kerja penting dalam bahasa Inggris yang sering digunakan dalam berbagai konteks. Materi ini akan membantu kamu mengenal jenis kata kerja dan contoh penggunaannya.

ðŸ”¹ To Be (Am, Is, Are, Was, Were)

Kata kerja untuk menyatakan keadaan atau identitas.

  • I am a student.
  • She is happy.
  • They were at the park yesterday.
ðŸ”¹ To Have (Have, Has, Had)

Menyatakan kepemilikan atau sebagai kata bantu perfect tense.

  • I have a car.
  • She has two cats.
  • We had lunch an hour ago.
ðŸ”¹ To Do (Do, Does, Did)

Kata kerja bantu atau kata kerja utama aktivitas.

  • Do you like pizza?
  • He does not play tennis.
  • I did my homework.
ðŸ”¹ Modal Verbs

Untuk kemampuan, kemungkinan, izin, dan kewajiban.

  • I can swim.
  • You should study harder.
  • She might come tomorrow.
  • We must finish on time.
ðŸ”¹ Common Action Verbs
Kata Kerja Arti Contoh Kalimat
GoPergiShe goes to school every day.
ComeDatangThey come home late.
EatMakanWe eat breakfast at 7 am.
DrinkMinumHe drinks coffee every morning.
WriteMenulisI write emails daily.
ReadMembacaShe reads a book now.
SpeakBerbicaraThey speak English fluently.
ListenMendengarkanPlease listen carefully.
WorkBekerjaHe works at a bank.
PlayBermainThe kids play football.
ðŸ”¹ Common Stative Verbs

Jenis kata kerja yang tidak digunakan dalam bentuk continuous tense.

  • Like (suka)
  • Love (cinta)
  • Hate (benci)
  • Know (tahu)
  • Understand (mengerti)
  • Believe (percaya)
  • Want (ingin)
  • Need (butuh)
ðŸ”¹ Phrasal Verbs (Kata Kerja Frasa)
  • Look after: menjaga
    Contoh: She looks after her little brother.
  • Give up: menyerah
    Contoh: He gave up smoking last year.
  • Turn on/off: menyalakan/mematikan
    Contoh: Please turn off the lights.
  • Take off: lepas landas / melepas
    Contoh: The plane took off at noon.
